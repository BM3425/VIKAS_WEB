Who We Are

The starting of this School by the YMCA Trivandrum, is to give a difference in our mission and vision to empower the poor and marginalized people in our community. The school which started with 5 children and a willing teacher has now grown to the nursery and presently caters to 130 children and 10 staff members coming from different localities in Trivandrum.

What we do

Our aim is to develop holistic personality among students by offering them opportunities for participation in various curricular and co-curricular activities. This approach is centered around comprehensive development that encompasses intellectual growth, emotional intelligence, physical well-being, and social skills.
