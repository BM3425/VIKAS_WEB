Partner With Us

Join Hands With Us

Vikas School, YMCA Tiruvalla, Kerala

Join hands with us and be a partner in this noble task. Let us share the joy of mitigating the suffering of at least a few people around us. Let us light a candle instead of blaming the darkness. You can be a part of this noble venture:

  • By giving a donation to sponsor any part of the project.
  • Patron - of the school (Rs. 10 Lakh and above)
  • By building a room in the proposed building, honouring your dear ones (Rs. 2 Lakh)
  • Benefactor - of the school (Rs. 5 Lakh and above)
  • Well-wisher - of the school (Rs. 1 Lakh and above)
  • By instituting scholarships or endowments in memory of your beloved ones.
  • By sponsoring one student for an year (Rs. 5,000 per year).
  • The names of all donors who contribute Rs. 25,000 or more will be inscribed on tablets.

We are confident that 'the good men on earth' will wholeheartedly support this noble mission undertaken by the Tiruvalla YMCA to serve the less privileged as well as mentally challenged children around us. Let us glorify our good Lord this way.
